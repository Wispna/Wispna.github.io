Glossary

A

The Abyss

Stare into it and you’ll fall. It is the deep deep water. It is one of the three regions of Yuani.

Abyssal Merfolk

They are the most alien mermaids. They look very monster.

Adora

The goddess of Love

Aetherius

The current emperor of Andaras. He is impotent.

Aeyoqia

Capital of Yuani

Alorel

Goddess of justice and mercy. She is worshipped primarily in Andaras and Kessa.

Alorin

Capital of Andaras

Andaras

The largest and strongest region in Tiranthe. It has massive political influence. Once a small region, it began an imperial campaign of conquest many years ago. It has, however, been quiet for the last few hundred years.

Aplens

Capital of Dreto

B

​Bao-rha

The god of valor

Blessing

A stat that determines how pious your character is and helps multiply damage/healing.

C

Chakir

Capital of Scatha.

Chaos Being

Chaos Beings are creatures that have lived in the chaos realm for eons, and many have gained sentience and immense power.

Chaos Realm

The Chaos Realm (also known as Methurin) is a dimension that rests besides our own. It has many similarities to the dimension Tiranthe is in, but the laws of nature aren’t the same. The Chaos dimension is also shattered into pieces.

​Cores

They are from golems. Don’t worry about it.

Cyrilis

They are pale elves.

Curse Mark

A mark left behind by The Priestess in her final hours. It mysteriously started appearing on people’s bodies across the continent.

D

Daiyur

A kingdom of Scatha, often mistaken for the capital.

Daran

​The Kessan Currency.

Dethi & Lealia

Two separate regions with closely entwined history. They were once one region as Dethi, but Lealia declared independence over a century ago. They are famous for a feud between two prominent families in each region, the Contagues and the Mapulets.

Dragons

They are magical large beasts.

Dreto

The human-dominated region on Tiranthe. It is a rich, beautiful region known for its entertainment and festivals. Its political landscape is peculiar as politicians frequently bribe the public for their favours with festivals and entertainment.

E

Elementals

They are humanoid elements.

Elves

They are pointy-earred bastards.

F

Fakian

God of deception, chaos, and apologies.

Feroxi

They are animal-earred racists.

Forra Tia

Capital of Triem Sha

G

Golems​

Magically constructed servants of the wealthy.

Gramhara

God of passion.

​Gwydion

The holy exarch of Andaras, AKA The Pope. He is also impotent.

H

Half-Blood

Half-blood characters are characters that have parentage from two separate races. Due to social stigma, and the rarity of half-bloods being able to be born in the first place, half-bloods are an uncommon sight.

Human

They are humans.

I

Ishari

An expansive region that is home to the Xanthi. Rolling grasslands stretch across this region.

J

K

Kalze

Capital of Dethi

Keeper of The Lost

A discreet deity who welcomes lost children.

Kessans

A variant of humans from Kessa.

Khyuhaya

Title of the elected leader of Yuani.

​Kessa

A large region covered in fae forests. It is known for producing many esteemed scholars and mages, but also for its dangerous fae population. Its people highly value diplomacy and world travel. Raehn is in this region.

L

​Dethi & Lealia

Two separate regions with closely entwined history. They were once one region as Dethi, but Lealia declared independence over a century ago. They are famous for a feud between two prominent families in each region, the Contagues and the Mapulets.

Luwaria

The goddess of water, the moon, and knowledge. She is primarily worshipped in Yuani, Qeyon, and Seyuna.

M

Maera

A princess of Andaras.

Malverians

They are a variation of elves that have crystals on their bodies and different coloration.

Merfolk

They are mermaids and merbutlers. They have aquatic tail, no fish head.

Methurin

Another name for the Chaos Realm.

Midaros

Popular port town in Andaras

Mido

Goddess of hearth, procreation, and parental love.

Myrenn

An important city where world leaders meet. It is home to the priestess and formally independent/neutral, but it has been under Andaran influence for 500 years.

N

Nahite

God of fortune, luck, art, and luxury.

Nakallas

God of humor

Nhysannia

The Goddess of the Vieru Swamp that borders Andaras and Kessa. She's not worshipped anywhere else.

The Night of a Thousand Veils

A festival descended from fae origins celebrated on Ghostlight 31. Festivalgoers don masks and costumes to evade fae notice as well as exchange treats.

Nirios

It is a neutral territory full of pirates. It is one of the largest ports on the continent and is a center for trade. The local pirates often clash with the merchant guild, which is the largest in Tiranthe.

O

Open Ocean

This is the middle water. It is one of the three regions of Yuani.

Open Ocean Merfolk

They are stranger mermaids. Very big.

P

Pact Marks

Pact marks are tattoos of black ink on summoners' bodies formed when they make a pact with a chaos being. When they summon their creature in its true form to their dimension, the pact marks will emit a smoke, cloud, or mist of their elemental affinity.

Pilgrimage

A one year journey across Tiranthe that worshippers of Sindariel must undertake. It is grim.

Port Farfall

The largest trading port in Qeyon.

The Priestess

The holy priestess who communes with the gods. She is a highly important religious figure across many faiths. She is known to have divine powers.

Q

Qeyon

Qeyon is a trade province in Yuani mostly run by mermaids. It is the only mermaid-occupied territory that is easily accessible to the masses.

R

Raehn

Once a holy city dedicated to the gods, it now sits abandoned in ruins.

Ransaros

A pirate town in Nirios that is surrounded by cliffs.

​Rinnea

Former capital of the elves that lived in Andaras.

S

The Sacred One

A blessed member of the Vieru village Urioh. They were able to leave the swamp without being bound to it. Currently deceased.

Sailor's Mark

This mark, gifted to the descendants of 6 sailors from long ago, allows characters with it to breathe underwater, as well as grants them slight aquatic features. This mark also grants characters abilities when they're beneath water depending on where it's located on their body.

Saiphina

Goddess of nature and creation.

Scatha

A region primarily occupied by dragons. It has wildly varying biomes across the island. Its two most notable cities are Chakir and Daiyur.

Seaside Xanthi

They are Viking furries.

Selpharas

The rocky, frozen, southern region that is home to Sindariel and her death clan.

Selpharim

Capital of Selpharas.

Senyan University

The largest magical university in Tiranthe. It exists in Seyuna and accepts all races, unlike those racist universities elsewhere.

Sercella

Capital of Lealia

Seren

The current Queen of Myrenn.

Seyuna

An above water city-island for mermaids which is used for diplomacy. It is near the capital of Yuani, Aeyoqia. It has the famous Senyan University which is many people on the continent travel to for study.

​Sha

The people of Triem Sha. They are smol.

The Shallow Isle

A mysterious island in the north. Little is known about it.

Shen

Capital of Ishari

Shenedorei

Capital of Wafetia

Shenra

Capital of Kessa.

Shore

This is the warm water, closest to land. It is one of the three regions of Yuani.

​Shore Merfolk

They are the classic mermaid.

Sindariel

The goddess of death. She is primarily worshipped in Selpharas. She is the eternal leader of Selpharas.

Solq

The god of sun, deserts, and hope. He is primarily worshipped in Scatha and Wafeitia.

The Statue of Change

A strange statue of a woman in the Vieru village of Urioh worshipped by the swamp cultists nearby. Anyone who approaches the statue will lose their mind and be changed into a bloodthirsty beast.

Streiya

Goddess of fate and stars.

Stonehawk

A xanthi guard of Queen Seren in Myrenn.

Summoners

People who choose to form pacts with chaos beings from the chaos realm in order to channel their powerful abilities.

Summoning Magic

Magic that conjures beings from the chaos realm through a pact.

T

Teleporter

A Portal device created using a mixture of sygaldry and old technology. It is ancient technology with few artifacts that have survived to the modern age. Technicians in Malveria are currently conducting research.

Therin

The currency of Andaras.

Thyta

Capital of Nirios

Tiranthe

The continent our group takes place on.

​Triem Sha

A hot desert region home to many predators. Most of the population live in small towns and are known for their peculiarly small size. They are also famous for their delicious cuisine.

The True King

There are rumors of a true king in Myrenn who is destined for the throne.

Torn

God of misfortune.

U

Urioh

A remote village in the swamp of Vieru. The villagers do not welcome strangers.

V

Vieru

Deadly swamp to the northwest of Andaras.

​Vitaria

The original empress who began Andaras’ conquest of Tiranthe. She is dead now and elves hate her.

W

​Wafetia

The last elf-dominated region in the north. It is famous for its massive trees and for being a bastion of elven knowledge. It is protected by the Mara Mountains to the south and a deadly swamp to the east.

X

Xanthi

They are furries.

Xianian

Shore Capital of Yuani.

Xhiro

Stinky

Y

Yuani

The underwater kingdom of the mermaids. It is the largest region in Tiranthe and no one truly knows how large it is or how deep it goes.

Yuns

The currency of Yuani

​Yuyoni

Open Ocean Capital.

Z

Zemonyi

The Abyss Capital.
