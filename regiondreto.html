Dreto


Region Name: Dreto

Capital City: Aplens

Primary God: Gramhara & Nahite

Races: 70% human, 30% everything else.


Description: Dreto is a place where decadence thrives, in postcard worthy sandy beaches and jade green fields. Artists and craftsmen often flock to this region due to its bustling markets and economic prosperity. This region is highly populated and is considered the heart of human civilization, although it welcomes any and all races within its borders.

The Political landscape in Dreto is a peculiar game of bribery, where the reigning oligarchs retain or lose their positions and affluence through buying the public's affection in forms of feasts and festivals, and commissioning poets and performers for public events across the region. As such, election years are quite popular in Dreto and politicians that are elected are usually well liked, albeit for often the wrong reasons.

Primary

Region Name: Dreto

Capital/Notable Cities: Aplens

Government Type: Oligarchy

Population: The largest human region on the continent, densely populated

Population Type: 70% human, 30% everything else.

Primary God: Gramhara and Nahite

Other Popular Gods Solq, Nakallas

Major Landscapes: Numerous rivers, grasslands

Major Resources: Fresh water, lumber, produce, food, entertainment.

General Climate: Warm and temperate in the spring, hot and muggy in the summer, cool and crisp in the autumn, and cold with snowfall in the winter. It experiences four seasons.

Influence Over the World: As the human capital of Tiranthe, it holds significant sway on the continent. The sheer large population probably helps along with its rich resources. It’s also a popular tourist location. ​



Secondary

How the rest of Tiranthe views them: It’s seen as a neutral territory with beautiful landscapes and a great place for a vacation. Artists love going there as a good story is always greatly valued.

Issues this country faces: It’s sandwiched right in the middle of Tiranthe between powerful players which makes it a strategic location to conquer.

What’s the economy like?: Very good. Its location also means it has access to many different regions for trade.

What are the main languages spoken?: “Common”, there are surprisingly quite a few people who speak Elvish.

Where do most people live?: In the cities. There are still many people who live in the farmlands who produce food for the entire continent.

How does this country view magic?:

How technologically advanced is this country? Is magic preferred?: It’s welcomed. It’s not viewed as greater than traditional technology, but as part of everything.

How is history recorded?: In great artwork primarily, such as tapestries and plays, but also in books.

How many are educated, and how?: Those in the cities are generally literate, especially for voting purposes, but the aristocrats are the most well-educated.

How did the majority of current people come to be on this land?: There is a good mix of people who have been here for generations, but it also tends to have many people who are displaced from their homeland or are looking for opportunities to better their lives.

Why do people choose to live here?: They have really good entertainment. Also, an opportunity to live a better life.

How popular is the government?: Quite popular because they have a tendency to bribe the public with band-aid solutions or fancy parades.

How are criminals charged and punished?: Criminals, no matter the gravity of their crimes, are all put into the coliseum system. In the coliseum, they are given the chance to fight to the death to clear their name (for the entertainment of all the masses who watch).
