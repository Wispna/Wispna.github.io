Monsters

There are many monsters throughout Tiranthe. You can see their details through the bot on the discord if you know their name. Names of previous monsters will be written here to serve as an aid for creating new monsters.

The !monst <monstername> command will be used to view monsters. Though the name isn't case sensitive, special characters such as _ must be included (if they're in the name) to be viewed in the bot properly.

Notes: A monster's essence shows how favored they are by the gods. Divine means they are particularly favored by one or more gods, and you will lose rep for killing them, but gain rep for sparing them. Neutral means they were simply a hassle and you may gain a small amount of rep for killing them or sparing them. Profane means they are disliked by most gods, and you will gain a decent amount of rep for killing them, but you will lose rep for sparing them.

Run # in the notes of some monsters is the recommended amount of monsters to run against a group of 4 characters. It is simply a guideline that is helpful to follow.
