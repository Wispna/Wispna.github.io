Qeyon

Region Name: Qeyon

Capital City: -

Primary God: Luwaria

Races: 50% Mermaids, 50% anything else.


Description: Qeyon is a majorly mermaid-run land area in Yuani. There are many trade cities that are dotted across the area, but the largest trade port there is Port Farfall. Most people travel there to buy pearls, dyes, coral, exotic medicine, gemstones, and, if youâ€™re wealthy, core stone for golems. This is the only mermaid province that is easily accessible to the masses, since Seyuna is more troublesome to get to as an island. Residents live further inland while shops are located around the shore. Qeyon, along with Seyuna, are the two areas where Shore mermaids reside if they choose to live on land. Qeyon is moderate in temperature, and is rich in vegetation from all the nearby rainfall that passes through Seyuna.
