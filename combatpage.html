Combat

What do I do during combat?

Our combat system involves opponents taking turns to attack, defend, and heal against each other until the opposing side is reduced to 0 HP or incapacitated. We use bots to roll d6 dice to determine this.

Vital Stats

HP: During combat, keep track of your HP. Each point of VIT is 1 point of HP.

> What does this mean for me?

    -If you have 6 Vitality, you will need 6 SUCCESSES against you to be beaten*

*If you don’t heal or defend yourself



Initiative: A fancy word for turn order. You roll your PHYS stat and add the total together. The higher the score, the faster your turn comes.

> What does this mean for me?

    -If you have 5 PHYS, you roll 5d6. EX. You roll and get the numbers (2, 4, 3, 1, 6). Your initiative would be 16.

    -BOT COMMAND: !init

What can I do during my turn?

Attack roll: You roll PHYS atk or SORC atk. Every 5 and 6, which is a success, counts as 1 point of damage onto your target. Roll your blessings along with your attacks when rolling.

    -Physical Attack: Every point in PHYS is how many d6’s you can roll to inflict damage. Any bonus rolls come from the Physical Tree branches you take or equipment.

> What does this mean for me?

    -If you have 4 in PHYS, you roll 4d6 when doing a Physical Attack.



    -Sorcery Attack: Every point in SORC is how many d6’s you can roll to inflict damage. Any bonus rolls come from the Magic Tree branches you take or equipment.

> What does this mean for me?

    -If you have 4 in SORC, you roll 4d6 when doing a Sorcery Attack.*

*You need Manipulation in an element to attack



    -BOT COMMAND: !atk <skill number> <blessings number>



Healing: On your turn, if you have healing capabilities, you can Heal instead of Attack. Roll SORC. Every success is 1 point of healed HP on your target. Roll your blessings along with your defenses when rolling.

> What does this mean for me?

    -You have 5 points in SORC, you roll 5d6 when healing.

    -BOT COMMAND: !heal <sorcery number> <blessings number>



Defense Roll: In addition to attacking or healing, you can defend yourself. You don't have to wait for your turn, you can defend any time someone is attacking you, but only once per round. This resets when the turn order restarts, and you can use your SORC or PHYS stat. For every success, deduct one success from the enemy’s attack. Remember to use your blessings along with your defenses when rolling.

> What does this mean for me?

    -An enemy attacks you with 4 successes. You immediately react with a defense roll and get 3 successes. You take 1 damage.

    -BOT COMMAND: !def <skill number> <blessings number>

Pray for Help

Blessings: BLESS is different from the other stats. This is an assisting stat. Blessings can boost your attacks, defenses, and heals. This ONLY happens when you roll a 6 on your blessings roll. Each success, your action is multiplied. A single success is x2, and every blessing after it increases the multiplier by 1.

> What does this mean for me?

    -You roll to attack. You roll 6 of your attack dice, and 3 of your blessing dice. Your attack is (5, 4, 6, 5, 3, 6), so you succeed 4 times. Your blessing is (6, 4, 6), so you succeeded 2 times. Your total damage is 12.*

*No need to get caught up in the math, we have a bot that will be doing this for you.
