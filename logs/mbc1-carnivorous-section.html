dunplings 02/03/2023 9:42 PM
This section is far in the back of the glass dome, and is full of plants that tend to have... monstrous tendencies. Many of the plants are large and alien looking. Many have thorns, teeth like appendages, and glue like traps in their anatomy. Only the most eccentric of gardeners work here, and they seem amused that any volunteer gardener would want to try to care for these plants at all. Fortunately the plants don't seem to spontaneously attack. One just has to know how to avoid triggering their carnivorous instincts. Working here will require making certain no unassuming visitors wander in, being very careful around the plants when tending to them, and feeding plants various diets of frozen rats, fish, insects, or in the cases of larger ones, steaks.
