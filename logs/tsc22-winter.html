Glass heels wedge into ice as she presses onto the frozen surface of the lake. Mirth dances in golden eyes as she watches on the scene in amusement of the snowfall. Sin is no beauty of swan lake, nor will she shed black feathers as she dances to lure the Prince into the waters... but she might as well play the part. A head of gold is replaced by a crown of glassy crystals, transparent yet eye catching all the same. Where she walks, she waltzes on her lonesome. A little tap here, and a little tap there. Ice that cracks slightly where she lands on her feet, but she does not fear any potential risk of the lake falling beneath her feet. The stage is set for her act, and a soundless melody rings in her head. Where there is still an area that remains unfrozen by a sheet of ice, she sticks her hands unflinchingly into the pool. A soft glow emits underwater... Perhaps she is searching for something in the murky depths.
