Rules: In a snowball fight, two opposing teams go against each other for victory. To achieve victory, they must hit every member on the opposing team with a snowball successfully. As soon as someone is hit with a snowball, they are out for that particular game. For every game won, a point for the winning team is earned. To determine who goes first, each team nominates a person to roll Init. Whichever team rolls the highest Init goes first. This is done every round. Alternatively, if it makes sense in RP, a team may attempt a sneak attack. In this case, both teams roll init, but instead of using phys for the amount of dice, use lore. If one team member has moderate dark sense, a sneak attack will not work against them. If a sneak attack is successful, the team that lost against the sneak attack may not use abilities for the first attack, and can only defend against the first attack against them. A sneak attack can only be done on the FIRST round. Once the order is determined, roll ability cards. Teams will start secretly rolling cards using the <!snowball> command in their special team channel. These cards will grant your team bonuses for the round. A team can roll and use as many cards as there are members on the OPPOSING team. So if one person was fighting against 4 people, they could use 4 cards, while the opposing team could use one card. If your team already has cards, then only roll as many cards as you used the last round. (E.g. if there are 4 members on the other team and you used one card last round so you’re only holding 3, only roll one more card so that you remain with a total of 4.) From there, your team may use as many of the abilities listed below as they wish (though NOTE that each ability may only be done ONCE per team. These abilities must also be skipped in the first round if your team lost against a sneak attack.):
17:44
1. Take Cover: Roll ACA. If you roll a success of 2 or greater, then your team successfully takes cover. When the opposing team next attacks you, they must attack with disadvantage (roll twice, and take the lower of the two rolls). 2. Burst of Energy: Roll VIT. If you roll a success of 2 or greater, then your team may attack with advantage (roll twice, and take the higher of the two rolls). If they are inflicted with disadvantage from take cover, then the advantage/disadvantage cancel out. 3. Guard: Choose someone on your team to guard. This may be chosen in your secret team channel. If you choose to do this action, your character is not allowed to attack this round. If the opposing team chooses the person you are guarding to attack, you may defend against the attack instead, and you may counter attack. You cannot guard multiple people on your team.
17:45
Once this is done, you may choose someone on your team to attack with a snowball. The team with the highest init attacks first. There may be only one attack per team per round. To attack, declare who you are attacking, then roll an attack against them with sorc or phys. Note, however, you may not use any bonuses from abilities or weapons. If the defense fails, then that character is out of the game, and may not use any rolls or abilities to assist their team further (though they can still rp stuff as moral support). Whoever is attacking will then roll a defense using sorc or phys– this defense may also not use any bonuses from abilities or weapons. Once both teams have done their attacks, then the round starts over. This repeats until one of the teams is completely out of the game. Once a team wins, use the <!addpoint teamname> command. (e.g. if team ireth wins, use the command <!addpoint ireth>. Points for both teams can be seen with the <!complb> command. (edited)
