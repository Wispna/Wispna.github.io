dunplings 02/03/2023 9:32 PM
This section of the botanical garden is filled with a variety of plants all known for their medicinal or alchemical uses. It is not open to the general public, and requires approval from a head gardener before entry is allowed. A pungent scent drifts through the air, and some of the plants seem to have magical quirks that can make them difficult to deal with. Some of the plants are kept in large underwater tanks, so those with talents for breathing underwater and swimming are appreciated. Working here requires good memorization of where different types of plants are to assist the alchemists and healers that need such services, and a good eye to make certain no enterprising thieves or lost visitors are wandering within. Knowing how to harvest certain plants is helpful as well. (edited)
