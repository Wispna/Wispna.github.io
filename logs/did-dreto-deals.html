To successfully retrieve the artifact Contessina has hidden away in her vault. The vault is made entirely of a stone that is resistant to manipulation by mineral magic, and can even warn the guards and lock the doors if it senses mineral manipulation attempted to be used on it. The entrance is in Contessina's garden-- a locked trap door beneath Contessina's favorite tree. 2 guards are stationed there, and they change shifts every 3 hours at midnight, 3 am, 6 am, 9 am, noon, 3 pm, 6pm, 9 pm. There is also a hidden entrance in Contessina's cellar, though it's said that one is bound by a magical lock that can only be opened from a key that Contessina wears around her neck or else it will make a loud sound. This entrance, however, will take you straight to room 3. The guards here change shifts every 6 hours.
10:20 PM
Room 1. A long hall laid with tiles in a 3x10 grid. Random tiles and bricks will trigger pit traps, arrow traps, and spike traps. Room 2. 5 levers are in this room and they must be pulled in the correct order. Each lever is marked with different symbols. Heart, Coin, Sword, Mask, and Key. If the wrong lever is pulled, the doors will lock, and alarm will sound, and the room will start filling with water. Room 3. This room is filled with stone statues. 3 basilisks that are tame to Contessina roam this room. They are very quick, and meeting their eyes will turn you to stone. Room 4. 6 Massive golems have a steadfast guard over this room. They will try to attack you as soon as you enter. It's said Contessina has a device that calms them. Room 5. This room has many holes in the walls that are filled with sapphire wasps. The venom from these wasps can be quite deadly and painful, though they recognize Contessina's scent and know not to attack. Room 6. This room is filled with treasures. Room 7. The artifact (edited)
10:24 PM
There is no 'correct' way to steal the artifact. Feel free to come up with any way you can think of to bypass vault security. Money goes a long way in Dreto. You can trade Fiorin marks you earn from quests for information, goods, and services-- the amount depends on what you ask for. I will trade any fiorin marks you spend for stone suns so you'll still have event currency. Feel free to use this room to plan, or to trade Fiorin Marks for something.
