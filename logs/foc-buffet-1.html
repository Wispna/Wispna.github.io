The center of the town is truly a sight to behold. Small magic-made stars light up the area, providing a soft, yet warm glow that makes it seem like the day remains. The structures surrounding it are lined with vines carefully grown around their frames. These vibrant and new vines are coated in flowers of all colours that give the town center a fresh and vibrant feel. Bright pinks, deep intoxicating reds, and blues the colour of the ocean’s depths somehow turn what was once a chipped and uncared for gathering point for the community into a truly dazzling display of light and nature. At the center of all of this, four rows of dazzling dishes stand created, creating an enticing aroma of gourmet foods. For some, these amazing and eloquent dishes are brand new, for others they’re lost treasures, the likes of which were given up on once they’d been thrown into Raehn. Best of all though, dishes from all over the continent are gathered. Comfort foods, home style meals, if you can imagine it, it's here! Best of all, the dishes everyone had worked on to create are here, waiting to be sampled by all! Behind each of these dazzling rows is a small, outdoor kitchen, ready for use should something run low on. Perhaps there will even be a chance to enjoy a dish freshly prepared by Kobe and Kim!
