Muscle Connoiseur || Conrad 18-Mar-21 09:56 PM
A man was walking by the edge of a pond. He was delivering some belongings back to his home to prepare something special for the feast proper. As he passed, he noticed another body, seemingly trying to catch fish... with their teeth. He shook his head and laughed, returning to the place he was meant to go. He returned a bit later, bearing two sticks with strings attached. He gently poked the other person, handing them a fishing rod in return. The sun was setting, and it was nearly time for the feast. Thankfully what the man was preparing was an ale that needed time to ferment, so he could spend the sunset hours with the other person. The other person spoke with their hands, which the man wasn't quite familiar with. He shrugged, and laid the fishing pole by their side while he cast his into the pond. The fish began biting, and the other decided to cast their line in as well. So they sat, spending the golden hours before the feast in quiet companionship, content.
