Make a wish! Your OC may be aware of a certain tradition done at festivals. It has different variants, depending on where you're from, or what gods you favor, however the tradition generally involves writing down a wish on a piece of paper and affixing it to a tree. Sometimes the tradition will involve folding the wish into shapes for good luck, and Streiya followers in particular tend to favor hanging the wishing on red strings under the starry night sky. Feel free to RP your OC partaking in a hopeful tradition. To also celebrate, I've pixeled a small base that you can follow or make your own.
