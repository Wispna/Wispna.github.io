NPCs

Main NPCs

Satchel

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: A man who has been lived in Raehn even before a curse mark brought in unexpected guests. You can often find him in the burial grounds, particularly if you have any glass sparrows you're looking to part with.

Ferrum

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: A correspondent from Myrenn with a distaste for the politicians there. Cares a lot about the Priestess.

Ferrum's Associate

Allegiance: Ferrum

Description: A quiet man usually seen with Ferrum.

Phileas

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: An eccentric elven researcher that's secretly a summoner. He knows a lot about occult topics, and is interested in the magical symbols found around Raehn. He is currently helping maintain the portal that's found outside the cathedral.

Finni

Allegiance: Phileas

Description: Phileas' assistant. Nya.

Kizhumie

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: A researcher brought from Malveria due to the curse, though she insists she was never cursed at all. She's currently helping maintain the portal found outside the cathedral.

Ireth

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: The water elemental guardian of Raehn. She resides in a pool near the tower.

Zafir

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: The fire elemental guardian of Raehn. He resides in the forge.

Glade

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: The nature elemental guardian of Raehn. He resides in the forest's edge. Those that fall unconscious there are brought back to safety by him.

Sevasha

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: The wind elemental guardian of Raehn. He resides in a windmill in the agricultural district.

???

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: Who is he? What is he? Where is he? Commonly referred to as 'Broomknight'.

Warden

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: One of the mysterious suits of armor that roams the forest. Something about it is different. Some call it 'Warden'.

Kael

Allegiance: Scatha

Description: A former dragon guard of Raehn who surrendered after the conflict.

Shikra

Allegiance: Yuani

Description: A former merfolk guard of Raehn who surrendered after the conflict.

Ocean

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: A water elemental in Triem Sha that was convinced to come back to Raehn during the Tales of Triem Sha event. He lives in the river near the falls, and is centuries old.

Doll

Allegiance: Raehn

Description: The unusually tiny golem that is the keeper of the strange clockwork place beneath the cathedral.



Regional NPCs

Seren

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: The bird feroxi queen of Myrenn. She is a descendant of the bird that was enstated after Andaras's invasion centuries ago. There aren't many who have faith in her rule.

Violetta Filvere

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: Violetta was adopted by Lord Navus, and was presented as the next priestess. Even Myrenn's holy artifact determined her as worthy, confirming that she has some sort of power connected with the priestess. Olnesia, however, later confirmed that Violetta was similarly experimented on in Rogeesin's magical laboratory, and perhaps altered in a way to make her fool the artifact.

Navus Filvere

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: An influential member of Myrenn's Holy Senate. The previous priestess changed his life, and he is currently the head of the faction that believes the priestess should reclaim her rightful rule over Myrenn. He adopted Violetta, and tried but failed to establish her as the new priestess due to uncertainty about her true identity.

Stonehawk

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: The wolf xanthi head royal guard of Myrenn. She is grateful to Raehn after they saved her and Queen Seren.

Camellia

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: The elven seeress of Myrenn. She was trapped in a deep sleep for decades due to an artifact used by Seren's father.

Charlumin

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: The no-nonsense owner of the Myrenn botanical garden.

Olnesia

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: Charlumin's assistant who was a part of magical experiments conducted in an illegal Myrenn laboratory.

Imlasca

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: Though she was an impoverished child, she managed to work her way up into becoming one of the wealthiest women in Myrenn. She is not only head of the Golden Leaf Trading Company, but a part of Myrenn's Holy Senate as well. She is neutral on the factions, neither speaking for the queen, the priestess, or the True King.

Ueyi

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: They are the pontifex of Myrenn's Church, as well as part of the Holy Senate. Ueyi is a strong traditionalist that believes Myrenn should return to its rightful rule under the priestess.

Leror

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: Leror is a former member of Myrenn's Holy Senate.He fell into disgrace after it came to light he murdered a fellow member of the Holy Senate, Palos, in cold blood. Leror is a secret supporter of the True King Faction.

Leror is currently in the Myrenn dungeons, and is in poor shape.

Rogeesin

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: A Myrenn noble that ran an illegal underground magical laboratory that experimented on orphans from an orphanage she heavily donated to. She is currently imprisoned in Myrenn.

Istaven

Allegiance: Ea'Shenar

Description: A promising member of the Ea'Shenar, an old group with contracts in many cities that has been resurfacing lately. He strongly believes in the prophecies of Sidaria, the elven seeress that had founded the Ea'Shenar, and believes these prophecies state that those cursed with the mark will bring about great ruin.

Arrash

Allegiance: Triem Sha

Description: The head of a merchant's caravan that helped the cursed in Triem Sha.

Ahmuhia

Allegiance: Triem Sha

Description: The cook of the merchant's caravan in Triem Sha. Was formerly a bandit.

Khodina

Allegiance: Triem Sha

Description: An ancient massive turtle, who created all turtles that live in Tiranthe, that lives deep in the azure oasis, and whose magic keeps it pure and fresh.

Trinn

Allegiance: Vieru

Description: Grew up in the swamp cult, but broke away to build her own life. She has unusual magic that involves manipulating a swamp flower that has potent sleep causing abilities.

Jirynpel Sal

Allegiance: Malveria

Description: An infamous Malverian researcher who had a theory magic was tied to memories. He believed he could bring back the memories of the dead, but his efforts failed. He then switched to researching zenosphinication, a malverian disease.

Sharanta Ytharzi

Allegiance: Malveria

Description: Jirynpel's researcher assistant. Her specialty is actually portals, but her old research group fell apart when Khizumie was imprisoned.



Deceased

Edgar

Allegiance: Andaras (Andaran emperor)

Description: The tyrannical head guard that terrorized Raehn while he was still alive. When the prisoners revolted, he decided he'd rather take his own life rather than be prisoner under those he used to rule.

Elizabht

Allegiance: Malveria

Description: A former guard of Raehn. Found dead at his post by the palace one morning.

Cygnus

Allegiance: Selpharas

Description: A former guard of Raehn. Killed after being kidnapped after a conflict with prisoners.

The Sacred One

Allegiance: Vieru

Description: The hope of Vieru. A person that could leave Vieru even though he was born there, which gave the others hope that they would one day be free from the cursed lands. Was changed into a monster by swamp cultists, then killed by the cursed.

Richter

Allegiance: Myrenn

Description: Believed in the rumors of a 'True King' of Myrenn. He tried to burn Queen Seren at the stake in a rebellion, but was stopped by the cursed. It was believed he committed suicide in prison, though strange dreams by the cursed hint otherwise.

Narseh

Allegiance: Triem Sha

Description: Was rescued by the merchant caravan run by Arrash, and became like family to them. He later betrayed them and poisoned the oasis, separating Ocean from Khodina. He then kidnapped Bejun, Ahmuhia's younger brother, trying to use Bejun as a new vessel for Ocean. His motives were unclear, but he mentioned it was needed to save the unknown god. Before he could say too much he combusted from within and burned to death.
