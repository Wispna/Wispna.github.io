Maps


KEY:

Walking: Each square represents 4 days of travel.

Riding/Sailing: Each square represents 2 days of travel.

Swimming (for merfolk, water dragons, and water elementals): Each square represents 2 days of travel.

Flying: Each square represents 1 day of travel.



NOTE:

These times are a baseline written with continuous travel in mind. Rest, or magic may change them.

Dragons and Feroxi can get exhausted during flight.

For dragons this is based on their physical. Each point in physical represents a day that a dragon can fly before complete and utter exhaustion.

For Feroxi this is based on their magic. Each point in magic represents half of a day a Feroxi can fly in their transformed state before complete and utter exhaustion.Â
