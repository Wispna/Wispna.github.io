Crafting

Crafting is now available for all characters! What's available to craft will be listed here.

To craft an item, use the <!craft itemname> command. If you have the necessary items in your inventory, it will automatically make it for you.

Seeing what's available may also be viewed in the bot with the <!craft recipes> command.

You may see what's exactly needed to craft the item desired with the <!item itemname> command. If the item is craftable, a recipe should be listed at the bottom.

Note: The weapon does not have to appear the same way it does in the pixel rendition when searched in the bot. In art you may draw the weapon differently/describe it differently in RP.
